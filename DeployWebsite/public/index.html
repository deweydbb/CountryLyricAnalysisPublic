Inspiration for this project

Grady Smith created this great video that analyzed the lyrics of the top 485 Country songs from the past five years. He collected the data for his video by hand and I knew I could use my programming skills to collect the lyrics of thousands of songs.

Much of the my inspiration for the project stems directly from this video.
